# Product Roadmap

## v2

- Supabase Auth integration with tenant-aware middleware.
- Full RBAC permission matrix and policy tests.
- Live Stripe Checkout, subscriptions, invoices, and webhook reconciliation.
- Twilio SMS inbound/outbound conversations.
- SendGrid/Mailgun campaigns with templates and unsubscribe handling.
- Google Calendar two-way sync and booking availability engine.
- OpenAI-powered assistants with prompt templates, tool calling, and audit logs.
- Drag-and-drop funnel/page builder with reusable sections.
- Form builder with conditional routing and attribution capture.
- Workflow builder canvas with branching, delays, webhooks, and retries.
- Client portal approvals, files, tasks, and reports.

## v3

- Marketplace for agency templates, funnels, automations, and dashboards.
- Advanced attribution and multi-touch revenue reporting.
- Data warehouse export and BI connectors.
- AI sales coach trained on agency playbooks and call transcripts.
- White-label custom domain automation with SSL provisioning.
- Multi-region support and enterprise SSO.
- Usage-based billing and tenant-level feature flags.
- Native mobile app companion.
