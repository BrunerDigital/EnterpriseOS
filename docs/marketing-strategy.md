# Marketing Strategy

## Positioning

Digital360 by BrunerDigital is the white-label AI business operating system for teams that need to capture, convert, manage, and report on growth from one platform.

## Primary ICPs

- Agencies packaging recurring SaaS-enabled services.
- Local service businesses that need faster lead follow-up and more booked appointments.
- Franchises and multi-location brands that need location visibility and brand governance.
- Consultants and coaches who want a premium client operating layer.
- Marketing teams consolidating campaigns, attribution, pipeline, and proof.

## Core Offer

Run CRM, pipelines, conversations, AI automation, calendars, funnels, reputation, reporting, billing, portals, and white-label controls from one premium workspace.

## Demand Generation Motions

- SEO pillar pages around white-label CRM, AI lead follow-up, CRM migration, agency client portals, reputation automation, and franchise marketing reporting.
- Paid acquisition to demo, audit, webinar, migration, and partner funnels.
- LinkedIn founder-led POV for agencies and growth operators.
- Retargeting to demo pages, case studies, and comparison pages.
- Partner program for agencies, consultants, and implementation partners.

## Conversion Architecture

Traffic routes to audience-specific landing pages, funnel offers, demo booking, agency applications, free trial signup, and lead magnet capture. Every form should create a CRM contact, capture UTM source, assign owner, trigger the correct nurture sequence, and send analytics events.
