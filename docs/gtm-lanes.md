# BrunerDigital GTM Lanes

## Lane 1: Agencies

Page: `/gtm/agencies`

Primary offer: apply for the white-label agency launch kit.

Conversion goal: qualified agency application and partner demo booking.

Use this lane for LinkedIn founder-led content, partner outbound, reseller conversations, and retargeting visitors who viewed white-label or agency pages.

Core message: turn your agency into a premium branded SaaS platform clients can log into, value, and expand.

## Lane 2: Local Services

Page: `/gtm/local-services`

Primary offer: free local lead leak audit.

Conversion goal: audit request followed by consultation or trial workspace.

Use this lane for Meta, Google Search, YouTube shorts, and outbound to service businesses with visible lead capture or review gaps.

Core message: convert more local leads before they go cold with speed-to-lead follow-up, booking, reviews, and reporting.

## Lane 3: Franchises And Multi-location

Page: `/gtm/franchises`

Primary offer: multi-location growth assessment.

Conversion goal: executive assessment submission and franchise demo booking.

Use this lane for LinkedIn, search, direct outreach, webinars, and ABM lists of franchise operators or regional brands.

Core message: give every location one growth system with executive visibility and brand-governed execution.
